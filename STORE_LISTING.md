## Detailed Description

RemindMeThat is a mind-wellness and productivity companion that lives in your browser. It combines a focus timer, ambient soundscapes, mood tracking, smart reminders, and screen time analytics into one calm dashboard.

FOCUS TIMER
Start a focus session from the dashboard with one tap. Choose 15, 25, 45, or 60 minutes. No navigation, no friction — just click and focus.

AMBIENT SOUNDS
8 sounds including Rain (real recorded audio with procedural drops), Forest (recorded birds and wind), White/Pink/Brown/Dark Noise, Cafe, and Ocean. Plays independently — not tied to the timer. Sound auto-pauses when you lock your screen and resumes when you return.

SMART REMINDERS
Gentle wellness nudges: drink water, breathe, stretch, eye rest. When you're away, reminders queue silently and show a single summary when you return. No notification spam.

MOOD TRACKING
One-tap daily mood check-in. Weekly mood charts, streak tracking, and simple insights over time.

PRIVATE JOURNAL
A personal notebook with serif font and privacy signals. Each entry links to your mood that day.

SCREEN TIME
Automatic tracking of productive vs. distraction time with categorized domains and spark charts.

TRACKER BLOCKER
Built-in ad and tracker blocking. Blocks Facebook Pixel, Google Analytics, TikTok Pixel, and more.

SITE BLOCKING
During focus sessions, distracting sites show a blocker overlay with time remaining.

SIDE PANEL
A companion panel that stays open while you browse — keep the timer visible alongside your work.

PRIVACY
All data stays on your device. No accounts, no servers, no analytics. Your data is yours.
